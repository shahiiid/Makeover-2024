Narayana Residence - Surat

"Narayana, a spacious 4bhk bungalow owned by Mr. Vasava, graces the urban landscape of Surat's vest area. This 2100 square feet abode underwent a remarkable transformation, focusing on a contemporary theme and an emphasis on spaciousness through turnkey solutions.

The interior design of Narayana was driven by a desire for simplicity and elegance. Initial challenges included an unsightly wall offset in the living area and towering beams. However, Mr. Shahid Patel's commitment to creating room height prevailed. Interior designers concealed the beams with a pelmet, seamlessly integrating them into the ceiling design. This pelmet served a dual purpose by concealing furnishings and illuminating wall artifacts. Additionally, a false wall with paneling discreetly concealed the wall offset.

The master bedroom adheres to the contemporary theme with a teak wood border, featuring a unique 45-degree groove design and clever storage integration. Glass-top side boxes add functionality and ease of maintenance. Vastu Shastra principles guided the decision to close a misaligned window without structural changes, allowing for the optimal placement of the bed. Wardrobe handles, designed with teak wood accents, and a carefully crafted niche add a touch of aesthetic appeal.

The entire project was executed with utmost care, addressing client requirements and surmounting design challenges seamlessly."
