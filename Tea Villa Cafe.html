Tea Villa Cafe - Ahmedabad

We take immense pride in our recent project for Tea Villa Cafe, strategically situated in the heart of Ahmedabad's most exquisite neighbourhood. Covering an expansive 1500 square feet, this project posed a unique challenge – how to revamp the space while preserving its inherent charm. Our expert designers rose to the occasion, opting for a minimalist theme that transformed the cafe into a haven of simplicity and elegance. At Makeover Design Studio, we believe in creating spaces that not only captivate the eye but also provide a welcoming and uncluttered environment for your patrons.
