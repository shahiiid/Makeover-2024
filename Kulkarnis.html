Mr. Kulkarni's  - Vadodara

"Explore Mr. Kulkarni's residence, a classic example of elegant design where each space exudes its unique identity. The house is tastefully designed, emanating a timeless and sophisticated charm. Every corner showcases creative elements that reflect the client's preferences.

Upon entering, the safety door captivates with exquisite leaf carvings and transparent sections. The adjacent name plate, featuring a natural scenic design, seamlessly complements the house's exterior theme.

Step into the glamorous living area, a focal point of balanced elements and symmetry. The backdrop boasts striking linear patterns, neutral hues, and dramatic wall lights. Royal molding borders add a majestic touch, while a central art piece, custom-designed by our interior experts, completes the decor. The distinctive couches and marble-topped coffee tables harmonize effortlessly, creating a captivating space. The TV wall echoes the royal borders, enhancing the overall harmony.

Moving to the dining area, it harmoniously aligns with the living space theme. The layered ceiling adorned with a delightful chandelier adds a charming ambiance. Custom-designed dining furniture adds a unique touch, while the kitchen maintains a classic, neutral aesthetic with innovative niches under the overheads.

The guest room offers a neutral and soothing atmosphere for a pleasant stay. A bed niche, complemented by a metallic texture wall, and brown leather accents on the bed create a welcoming vibe. Capsule-shaped handles on the closets add a unique touch, and a compact temple space is thoughtfully included.

The master bedroom defines opulence with its luxurious elements. The upholstered bed wall, featuring a beautiful blue hue, copper borders, and tinted mirrors, creates a marvelous effect. Hanging lights enhance the ambiance, and the tinted glass closet aligns seamlessly with the overall theme. A dressing area, complete with storage, caters to the client's needs, even incorporating Vastu Shastra principles.

In the kid's bedroom, playful design reigns supreme. An artist-painted bed wall with a scenic backdrop adds joyful colors to the room. Cleverly designed side tables and closets featuring teddy bear motifs make the space charming. A curved background study table completes the theme.

Finally, step onto the cozy balcony, designed perfectly for relaxation amidst greenery and stone. Functional metal shelves provide convenience for outdoor lounging."

Experience the epitome of design excellence at Mr. Kulkarni's residence.
