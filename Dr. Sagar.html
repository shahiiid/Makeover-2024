Dr. Sagar & Dhvani's

"At Makeover Design Studio, we believe in the power of design to express your identity. Take 'Swarg' a 1200 sqft Duplex we transformed for Mr. Sagar Bhai. Minimalism was our guiding principle, evident from the harmonious entrance featuring diagonal lines and a unique name plate. Inside, the foyer boasts spiritual phrases and a designer console, while the living area centers around a captivating curved wall with leather couches.

We reimagined the dining and kitchen with a classic brown palette, complemented by smartly chosen hanging lights. The master bedroom exudes modern coziness with blue and grey tones, featuring natural stone accents and a creative loft with paintings. The parents' bedroom radiates soothing elegance, with intricate wall patterns and well-designed wardrobes.

The guest bedroom offers tranquil simplicity, adorned with wooden paneling and dual-tone wardrobes. Our turnkey solutions ensured a satisfying transformation, thanks to our client's cooperation and trust in our expertise."
