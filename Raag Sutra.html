Raag Sutra - Ahmedabad

Raag Sutra is a Ahmedabad-based clothing label headed by Shobhit Shah who draws inspiration from motifs, Cultural and other elements that are part of the Classical culture. The brief, thus, was to use these as stylistic cues, taking care that they would not overshadow his sartorial creations. It also required everything unfinished and antique from the floor to the walls and ceiling. The design team took note of classical design elements reminiscent of Indian architecture such as Wood and curvilinear arches. The material palette mainly consists of royal ivory pallett, antique-finish wooden and clear looking mirror with real tree bark texture.
