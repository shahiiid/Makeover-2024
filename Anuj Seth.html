Mr. Anuj Seth's 

Vadodara

Mr. Anuj Seth's - 2 BHK

Makeover Design Studio, the best interior designer in Vadodara, transformed Mr. Seth's 2 BHK Apartment into a stunning showcase of minimalism and modernism. Our interior designers worked diligently to meet the client's requirements, resulting in a simple yet elegant space with turnkey solutions.

The living room, dining area, and kitchen seamlessly blend together with a clean and sophisticated design. The living area features stylish paneling and pastel couches with floral cushions, adding a touch of sophistication. The dining area boasts a brown-hued mirror and a custom-designed hanging light, enhancing its beauty. The kitchen uses subtle colors to create an illusion of more space.

The parent's bedroom exudes a modern Roman theme with cozy and enlightening hues. The dual-toned wardrobe adds to the room's appeal.

Moving to the upper floors, the master bedroom impresses with an Italian theme, featuring a luxurious bed back wall, a classy console design, and eye-catching art pieces. The brother's room takes a quirky turn with bold-colored textures and carefully chosen art pieces.

The guest room on the second floor offers simplicity and creativity, with patterned bed backs, matching wardrobe handles, and carefully selected paintings. An attached study table maximizes functionality.

Throughout the project, our interior designers worked closely with the client, providing a design that satisfied their vision with comprehensive turnkey solutions. Choose Makeover Design Studio for your interior design needs in Vadodara.
