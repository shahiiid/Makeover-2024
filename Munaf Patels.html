Mr. Munaf Patel's Residence

"Munaf Patel, the former Indian cricketer, has entrusted MAKEOVER DESIGN STUDIO with the transformation of his residence. This 4-bedroom villa, nestled in the suburbs of Ikhar - Bharuch, accommodates a family of 6 spanning three generations. Our design philosophy revolves around creating a harmonious and inviting living space that resonates with all family members. In common areas, we've employed a warm and muted material palette with elegant brass accents, ensuring inclusivity for every user. As you venture into more personalized private spaces, vibrant colors and textures come to life.

The brass and wood partition between the living and dining areas not only defines the spaces but also establishes a cohesive visual language for the entire common area. This screen serves as a striking backdrop for both the sitting and dining spaces while enhancing privacy within the apartment.

Moving to the bedrooms, the master bedroom exudes warmth through a blend of wood and beige tones, artfully combining solid and reflective surfaces. The parents' bedroom is adorned with soothing pastel blues, complemented by wood and shades of grey and white. For the daughters' bedroom, we've crafted a captivating outer space theme to cater to their deep interest.

Let MAKEOVER DESIGN STUDIO transform your residence into a personalized oasis just like we did for Munaf Patel's family. Contact us today for expert interior design services."
